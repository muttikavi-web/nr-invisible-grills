## ✨ On-Page SEO Elements

### Headings Structure (H1 → H6)
- **H1**: "Elevate Your Space With Sleek Security" (Hero section)
- **H2**: "Key Benefits of Invisible Grills", "Our Services", etc.
- **H3**: Feature titles, service names
- **H4**: Testimonial names, process steps

### Alt Text for Images
All images should include descriptive alt text:
```html
<img src="NRSecureProfile.png" alt="Professional NR Invisible Grills installation in Bangalore with clear balcony view" />
```

### Internal Linking
- Navigation links help distribute page authority
- Links use descriptive anchor text
- Example: "balcony safety grills" instead of "click here"

### URL Structure
Clean, descriptive URLs without unnecessary parameters:
- ✅ Good: `/balcony-invisible-grills`
- ❌ Bad: `/product?id=123&cat=grill`
